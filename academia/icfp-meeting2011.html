This is the webpage for the program committee meeting for ICFP 2011. You will find practical information needed to attend the meeting.

Getting to the PC meeting

It will take place in the building of INRIA Paris, near Place d'Italie. Here are the directions to go to INRIA and to your hotels:

Charles de Gaulle Airport → Place d'Italie Take the RER B, direction Paris, until Denfert-Rochereau. Take the metro line 6, direction Nation, get down at Place d'Italie.
Orly Airport → Place d'Italie Take the Orlybus until Denfert-Rochereau. Take the metro line 6, direction Nation, get down at Place d'Italie.
Gare du Nord → Place d'Italie Take metro line 5 to Place d'Italie.
Place d'Italie → PC meeting Follow the Avenue d'Italie (left sidewalk) until number 23, next to the post office (INRIA).
Place d'Italie → IBIS hotel Take rue Bobillot until rue de Tolbiac. You should see a church at the corner. Take a left in rue de Tolbiac for 150m.
Place d'Italie → Amiral hotel Follow the Avenue d'Italie (right sidewalk) until number 98 (500m).
Alternatively, to reach the hotels you can stop at Tolbiac rather than Place d'Italie (line 7): it is closer but only one line stops there.

Internet & printers

Unfortunately, you will have to ask for a daily wifi coupon every day at the reception (first floor).

Connect your laptop to the wifi "INRIA-Guest" and enter your username and password. Mind that "." or ":" at the begining of the username are part of it! There are 2 printers near the meeting room:

  • phIT564a : black & white duplex copier, can staple, print on A3 (Toshiba eStudio 523)
  • cIT564a : duplex color printer (Xerox Phaser 8560)
To use them, add them as remote printers:
  • Windows: Start > Printers and faxes > Add printer > Network printer > Connect to an internet printer: http://wificups-roc.inria.fr:631/printers/<name_of_printer> > Choose the appropriate driver
  • Mac OS X: System Preferences > Printers and faxes > Add printer > Protocol: IPP; Host: wificups-roc.inria.fr; Spool queue: /printers/<name_of_printer>
  • Linux: System > Administration > Printing > Add > Internet Printing Protocol (IPP); Host: wificups-roc.inria.fr; Spool queue: /printers/<name_of_printer>

Convenience

The neighborhood of INRIA offers quite a few convenience and distractions:
  • There is a supermarket (Carrefour) and a (pretty big for french standards) mall right in front of the entrance of INRIA's building. Just cross the street and enter the mall under the Carrefour sign.
  • One, if not the nicest swimming pool in Paris is located at five minute walk from INRIA and from your hotel:
    Piscine de la Butte-aux-Cailles 5, place Paul Verlaine 75013 PARIS
  • La Butte-aux-Cailles is a lively and pittoresque neighborhood where to eat or have a drink in the evening. Located west of INRIA and north of the Ibis.
  • Paris' Chinatown is located directly east from INRIA. Cross the parc de Choisy behind the building.
